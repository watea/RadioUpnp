<p><i>RadioUpnp</i> lets you enjoy web radios with uninterrupted playback.</p>

<p><b>Now with Google Cast support!</b> Stream your favorite radios directly to Cast-enabled devices, UPnP, or DLNA speakers — perfect for smart homes or audiophile setups.</p>

<p>Minimalist and fully customizable, you can easily build your own playlist:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Search and add radios directly in the app.</li>
  <li>Share results from RadioGarden straight to <i>RadioUpnp</i>.</li>
  <li>Still missing a station? Add it manually.</li>
</ul>

<p>Completely free and ad-free. Support development via the <b>Donate</b> menu. Thank you!</p>

<p>📌 <b>Troubleshooting Tips</b>:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Some phone brands (e.g. Samsung, OnePlus) may apply battery optimization that disrupts streaming. Disable optimization in battery settings for <i>RadioUpnp</i>.</li>
  <li>Streaming quality and compatibility depend on your UPnP/DLNA device (supported CODECs, etc.).</li>
  <li>If you use ad blockers or firewall apps, UPnP streaming may be blocked.</li>
  <li>Need help? Use the in-app Report function to contact the developer.</li>
</ul>
